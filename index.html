My toolbox

Here are some of the skills I have developed over my years of internships, side projects and student design teams.
I am proficient in solid modelling for a variety of manufacturing processes including forging, stamping, injection molding, and many others. Using my knowledge of GD&T, I have been responsible for creating and preparing hundreds of drawings for release.
I have experience with G2 and G3 surface modelling to create complex shapes. I also have over 5 years of solid modelling experience.
Using ANSYS Structural, I have analysed the behavior of metal and composite parts to ensure performance and safety requirements are met.
Leveraging my knowledge of VBA and macros, I can create powerful spreadsheets for tolerance stacks and other engineering calculations.
I have performed nodal, heat transfer, CFD, and structural analysis using SimScale to optimise musical instruments, battery enclosures, and vehicle components.
Through coursework and internships, I have learned how to model multi-link mechanisms and spring-damper systems with matlab.
To determine ideal CFD simulation parameters, I iterated through different Y+ settings, mesh elements, and turbulence models on a NACA airfoil to optimize for simulation speed and accuracy.
I have over 5 years of experience with AutoCAD producing drawings for a variety of 2 axis manufanufacturing methods.
